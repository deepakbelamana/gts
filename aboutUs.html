About us

GoTestSeries is an ed-tech startup which provides online test series for various competitive exams and hosts test series of coaching institutes. .

We provide a platform where students find their required test for minimal price. We also provide platform to coaching/collage/tuition/educator to conduct their tests online. any coaching/collage/tuition/educator can upload their test on GoTestSeries for free.

EARNINGS AND CHARGES

FOR FREE TESTS

We never charge any kind of fee for free tests. Any educator/coaching/collage/tuition can upload their tests for free without any restriction. Later, they may apply for GO ads for this they will receive 75% revenue made by those ads.
FOR PAID TESTS

We will charge 10% of test price as our service chages, rest 90% of test price will be deposited in your bank account. Compare our charges with Udemy.
REPORTING

Any coaching/collage/tuition/educator can track all information about tests including the date of purchase, student's username, total price paid and their revenue.
